Down Garment Cleaning

Washing

  • Rinse stains with down cleaner
  • Turn sleeping bag or jacket inside out
  • Pour warm water in bathtub
  • Soak 1 hour with down wash in tub
  • Do another bath cycle with no soap to clear out down wash
  • Carefully wring out as much water as possible

 

Drying

  • Carefully carry a wringed out bag to the dryer - it can easily tear. Grab from bottom
  • Large front loading dryer, set to low heat
  • Add tennis balls for agitation. If the item you are drying has velcro, don’t use tennis balls.
  • Make sure the item is tumbling when drying. You don’t want same part of garment in the same place in the dryer
  • Periodically go through items and manually break up clumps. This takes time.
  • Typically after each cycle, pull it out and massage out any down clumps
  • Make sure product is completely dry before storing
